The Ritz-Carlton, Toronto

An Icon of Contemporary Elegance in the Heart of Downtown Toronto

⭐ Overall Rating: 9.7/10

  • Design & Ambiance: 9.5

  • Service & Staff: 10

  • Rooms & Comfort: 9.6

  • Dining & Bars: 9.8

  • Wellness & Amenities: 9.5

  • Location: 10

  • Value for Luxury: 9.2

Hotel Snapshot

Perched in the city’s financial and entertainment district, The Ritz-Carlton, Toronto epitomizes modern Canadian luxury. With sweeping views of Lake Ontario and the CN Tower, an award-winning culinary scene, and refined service culture, this five-star hotel delivers an urban sanctuary for elite business and leisure travelers alike.

The property is part of the Ritz-Carlton global collection, but it stands out through its sophisticated Canadian identity, featuring locally inspired design, curated art, and a signature scent that immediately sets a serene tone upon entry.

Rooms & Suites: Contemporary Comfort Meets Panoramic Views

Each of the 263 rooms and suites is a blend of understated luxury and modern elegance. Expect floor-to-ceiling windows, marble bathrooms with soaking tubs, Frette linens, and Nespresso machines. Suites offer living areas ideal for entertaining or extended stays, while Club Level accommodations come with access to a private lounge serving five gourmet food presentations daily.

Highlight: The Corner Suite offers spectacular views of the CN Tower and a bath experience that feels almost cinematic.

Dining: Culinary Excellence with Canadian Flair

➤ TOCA – Italian Fine Dining with a Local Twist

Helmed by Executive Chef Paul Shewchuk, TOCA serves refined Italian cuisine using fresh Canadian ingredients. The standout feature is the TOCA Cheese Cave—the only hotel-based cheese cave in Canada.

  • Must-try: Handmade burrata, Nova Scotia lobster pasta, and the 24-month-aged Parmigiano from the cave.

➤ EPOCH Bar & Kitchen Terrace – Upscale Gastro-Lounge

A seasonal terrace, British-inspired cocktails, and a menu of elevated comfort food make EPOCH a social hub for locals and hotel guests. It's where Toronto’s luxury set gathers.

  • Signature Drink: The CN Tower Negroni — a visual and flavorful masterpiece.

Spa & Wellness: A Sanctuary Above the City

The Ritz-Carlton Spa, Toronto is one of the largest in downtown, offering 23,000 sq ft of serenity. Facilities include:

  • Saltwater lap pool with natural light

  • Eucalyptus steam rooms

  • Full-service fitness center with Peloton bikes

  • Signature treatments such as the Toronto Facial and Cloud 9 Massage

Exclusive Touch: Spa guests are offered house-made herbal teas and energy bites, enhancing the restorative experience.

Service & Personalization: Pure Ritz-Carlton Standard

Every guest interaction is polished yet warm. From personalized check-in to the unmatched concierge expertise, the staff delivers on the brand’s reputation for anticipatory service. Club Level service includes a private butler and curated city itineraries.

TurboRoo Tip: Celebrate a special occasion and staff will go out of their way with handcrafted amenities, personalized desserts, or rose petal turndowns.

Location: Downtown’s Cultural Crossroads

Perfectly positioned near:

  • CN Tower (2-minute walk)

  • Rogers Centre & Scotiabank Arena

  • PATH underground shopping network

  • Harbourfront Centre and Lake Ontario

Ideal for event-goers, art lovers, or luxury shoppers exploring Bloor-Yorkville.

What Sets It Apart

  • Cheese cave dining experience at TOCA

  • Largest luxury spa in downtown Toronto

  • Unrivaled views of both skyline and lake

  • Club Lounge with gourmet presentations

  • Architectural photography-worthy interiors

Final Verdict

The Ritz-Carlton, Toronto delivers an exceptional luxury experience that feels both timeless and Toronto-rooted. Perfect for discerning travelers who seek elegant design, world-class service, and proximity to the city’s cultural pulse.

  • Ideal For: Business travelers, luxury couples, event-goers, and culinary enthusiasts

    Not For: Budget travelers or those seeking boutique eccentricity
