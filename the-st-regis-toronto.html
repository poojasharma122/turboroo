Hotel Snapshot

Set in the heart of the Financial District, The St. Regis Toronto is the epitome of refined urban luxury. As Canada’s first St. Regis property, this soaring hotel delivers customized service, cosmopolitan design, and signature rituals that place it in a class of its own. With 258 spacious rooms and suites across 65 floors, it caters to discerning business travelers, luxury weekenders, and international elites seeking a sanctuary with views, elegance, and legacy.

From its signature Champagne Sabering Ceremony to its butler-serviced suites, every element of this hotel exudes quiet glamour and professional polish.

Rooms & Suites: Modern Comfort, Tailored Sophistication

All rooms at The St. Regis Toronto are generously sized and feature elegant, understated décor—rich neutral tones, textured fabrics, and handcrafted furniture.

Signature Perks:

  • Deluxe and Premier Rooms: Starting at 550 sq ft, with deep soaking tubs, Italian linens, and views of downtown Toronto.

  • Caroline Astor Suites & Executive Suites: Separate living spaces, marble fireplaces, and floor-to-ceiling windows.

  • St. Regis Suite Collection: Personalized butler service, bespoke amenities, and curated art pieces.

Top Pick: The Astor Two-Bedroom Suite—perfect for family luxury or high-level business travel, with its skyline views and private dining space.

Notable Features:

  • Signature St. Regis beds and Frette linens

  • iPad-controlled lighting and climate

  • Large marble bathrooms with rainfall showers

  • Plush robes and Remède bath products

Dining: Elevated Tastes in a Serene Setting

➤ Louix Louis – Sky-High Fine Dining

Located on the 31st floor, Louix Louis is known for its two-storey bar, grand ceiling murals, and refined North American cuisine with French influences.

  • Must-Try: 13-layer King’s Cake, bone-in ribeye, and the signature house martini

  • Ambience: Gold-accented glamor with skyline views; ideal for elegant brunch, power dinners, or late-evening cocktails

➤ Astor Lounge – Lobby-Level Serenity

A calm, light-filled space offering afternoon tea, artisanal coffees, and Champagne service. Quietly elegant and ideal for discrete meetings or personal relaxation.

Room Service: Available 24/7 with full Louix Louis menu access and custom in-room dining presentations.

Spa & Wellness: Elevated Wellbeing

St. Regis Spa – Urban Renewal Above the Clouds

This 31st-floor spa delivers a luxurious escape in the heart of the city. Expect:

  • Bespoke facials using Valmont and Swiss skincare lines

  • Signature massages designed for frequent travelers

  • Couples’ treatment rooms and deep relaxation spaces

Additional Amenities:

  • Heated infinity saltwater lap pool with panoramic views

  • Whirlpool, steam room, and full-service fitness studio with Peloton bikes

TurboRoo Tip: Book the Jet Lag Recovery Treatment upon arrival for a perfect posttravel reset.

Service & Signature Rituals

Service at The St. Regis Toronto is personal, intuitive, and proudly ceremonial.

  • Butler Service: Available for all suites—includes unpacking, pressing, coffee/tea delivery, and personalized itinerary planning.

  • Champagne Sabering: A daily ritual in the Astor Lounge that celebrates the St. Regis legacy in grand style.

  • Concierge: Expert in curating custom experiences—from private art gallery visits to exclusive restaurant reservations.

Location: Luxury in the Financial District

Centrally located at Bay and Adelaide, the hotel is within walking distance to:

  • Toronto Eaton Centre

  • Scotiabank Arena

  • CN Tower and Rogers Centre

  • PATH underground network

  • King West restaurants and Queen Street boutiques

Perfect for guests blending business and leisure, or seeking a luxurious home base to explore the city.

What Makes It Exceptional

  • Legendary St. Regis butler service

  • Skyline views from spa and pool on the 31st floor

  • Daily Champagne Sabering ritual and classic afternoon tea

  • Striking architecture and two-storey bar at Louix Louis

  • Spacious rooms and personalized in-room touches

Final Verdict

The St. Regis Toronto delivers classic luxury with a modern Toronto edge. It’s a hotel for those who value bespoke service, cosmopolitan aesthetics, and elevated experiences. With its towering views and signature rituals, it offers a serene counterpoint to the city’s energy below.

  • Ideal For: Luxury business travelers, couples, special occasions, international guests

    Not For: Travelers seeking boutique quirkiness or resort-style outdoor space
