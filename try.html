Best Casino Hotels Across Canada for Festival and Entertainment Travel

Looking for a festival trip that blends music, food, nightlife, and gaming? Canada’s top casino hotels offer world-class accommodations, on-site entertainment, and quick access to nearby festivals. Here’s a complete guide to the best casino hotels across the country.

Why Choose a Casino Hotel for Festival Travel?

Casino hotels offer more than gaming — they combine luxury stays, live performances, gourmet dining, spas, and nightlife, making them ideal bases for travellers attending nearby festivals. Plus, you’ll often find bonus events like concerts, comedy shows, or culinary weekends hosted right on-site.

Best Casino Hotels in Canada

Casino Rama Resort — Orillia, Ontario

Located north of Toronto, Casino Rama Resort combines a full-service hotel with a large casino, a spa, an indoor pool, and a concert venue hosting major international artists. It's a smart option for those attending events like Toronto’s Pride Festival or TIFF who want a relaxing escape after the city energy.

Highlights:
  • Over 2,500 slot machines and table games
  • Concerts, comedy shows, and live entertainment onsite
  • Full spa and indoor saltwater pool

River Rock Casino Resort — Richmond, British Columbia

Close to Vancouver and the Celebration of Light festivities, River Rock Casino Resort offers easy access to downtown while providing a luxurious waterfront stay. With an award-winning spa, several dining options, and live shows, it's a strong pick for festival-goers seeking entertainment and relaxation.

Highlights:
  • Largest casino in British Columbia
  • Luxury spa and fitness centre
  • Marina access and SkyTrain connectivity to downtown Vancouver

Fallsview Casino Resort — Niagara Falls, Ontario

Fallsview Casino Resort delivers a festival-like experience year-round with shows, shopping, and incredible views of Niagara Falls. It’s a smart choice for visitors attending local food festivals, concerts, or simply wanting a destination experience.

Highlights:
  • Direct views of Niagara Falls from many rooms
  • 3,000 slot machines and over 100 gaming tables
  • 1,500-seat Avalon Theatre for live performances

Casino de Montréal — Montreal, Quebec

Festival visitors attending events like the Montreal Jazz Festival or Osheaga can enjoy the added bonus of Casino de Montréal — one of the largest casinos in North America. Located on Île Notre-Dame, the casino offers vibrant nightlife, fine dining, and live shows, just a short ride from downtown Montreal.

Highlights:
  • Massive multi-level gaming and dining space
  • Regular live shows and themed party nights
  • Close proximity to Old Montreal and downtown festivals

Tips for Booking Casino Hotels During Festival Season

  • Book Early: Festival weekends and holiday weekends fill casino hotels fast.
  • Look for Entertainment Packages: Many casino hotels bundle gaming credits, show tickets, and dining discounts.
  • Balance Proximity: Some casino resorts are outside downtown cores. Double-check transport options if you're planning to move between the festival and hotel frequently.
