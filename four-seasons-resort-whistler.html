Four Seasons Resort Whistler

Hotel Snapshot

Set against the backdrop of British Columbia’s stunning Coast Mountains, Four Seasons Resort Whistler redefines luxury in Canada’s premier ski destination. Blending rugged alpine charm with the brand’s signature sophistication, this five-star mountain retreat delivers world-class service, slope-side access, and a sense of warm seclusion—all within minutes of Whistler Village.

Designed with natural materials, cozy grandeur, and understated opulence, the resort is equally suited to romantic getaways, family vacations, or solo wellness escapes.

Rooms & Suites: Warmth, Space, and Mountain Views

The 273 accommodations are among the most spacious in Whistler, featuring handcrafted wood furnishings, cozy gas fireplaces, and private balconies overlooking snow-dusted peaks or lush forest.

Signature Perks:

  • Standard Rooms: Start at 550 sq ft, with deep soaking tubs and heated marble floors.

  • Suites & Residences: Include full kitchens, private terraces, and multi-bedroom layouts ideal for families or extended stays.

Highlight Suite: The Two-Bedroom Premier Suite offers panoramic views, a dining room, fireplace lounge, and walk-in closets—perfect for travelers who want to live the alpine lodge dream in full luxury.

Dining: Elevated Comfort with a Canadian Twist

➤ Braidwood Tavern – Alpine Tavern Meets Artisanal Pub

The resort’s main restaurant delivers approachable luxury: local craft beers, foraged ingredients, and warm mountain décor. Think bison short ribs, truffle fries, and BC wine flights

➤ Sidecut Steakhouse – Whistler’s Upscale Classic

Sleek and bold, Sidecut specializes in aged Canadian Angus beef, fresh seafood, and classic cocktails. The modern mountain design makes it as visually impressive as it is satisfying.

  • Must-Try Dish: Dry-aged striploin with fire-roasted seasonal vegetables

  • Best Seat: Beside the fire on the terrace, post-ski with mulled wine

Spa & Wellness: Nature-Infused Rejuvenation

The Spa at Four Seasons – Luxury Wellness with Alpine Soul

The full-service spa offers treatment rooms with mountain views, signature massages using local botanicals, and post-treatment access to outdoor whirlpools under the stars.

  • Recommended: Après-Ski Leg Reviver massage and the glacial clay detox wrap

The heated outdoor pool and three whirlpools, surrounded by evergreens and steam rising into crisp air, create a cinematic wellness moment year-round.

Service: Polished, Personal, and Proactive

The staff sets an exceptionally high standard, with a warm, small-town hospitality feel wrapped in global luxury polish. From personal ski valets to trail-side hot cocoa deliveries, every interaction is thoughtful, efficient, and intuitive.

Families are especially well-cared for, with kid-friendly amenities, child-sized robes, and activity kits.

TurboRoo Tip: Ask the concierge to arrange a private heli-skiing tour or snowmobile adventure—tailored itineraries are where this property shines.

Location: Slope-Side Serenity with Village Access

While not directly on the slopes, the hotel offers:

  • A complimentary shuttle to Whistler Blackcomb gondolas

  • Private ski concierge service to store and prep equipment

  • Easy access to Whistler Village’s restaurants, shops, and nightlife

For guests seeking both quiet luxury and ski-town culture, this location is ideal.

What Makes It Exceptional

  • Spacious alpine-style rooms with fireplaces and balconies

  • Outdoor heated pool and whirlpools surrounded by snow or forest

  • Personalized ski concierge and gear valet

  • Family-friendly amenities with luxury consistency

  • Private residences available for longer or group stays

Final Verdict

Four Seasons Resort Whistler delivers a uniquely Canadian luxury experience that blends refined alpine charm with urban-level sophistication. It’s a hotel that invites you to slow down, breathe in the crisp mountain air, and immerse yourself in cozy indulgence without ever sacrificing modern convenience.

  • Ideal For: Luxury ski trips, wellness escapes, families, nature-loving couples

    Not For: Urbanites looking for nightlife-first stays or edgy boutique design
