Rosewood Hotel Georgia, Vancouver

Heritage Elegance Meets Contemporary Sophistication

Hotel Snapshot

Originally opened in 1927 and painstakingly restored in 2011, Rosewood Hotel Georgia is a masterclass in historic luxury. Located directly across from the Vancouver Art Gallery and Robson Square, this hotel fuses Roaring Twenties glamour with 21st-century polish, offering one of the most elegant and quietly indulgent stays in the city.

Its boutique feel, central location, and impeccable service make it a favorite among cultural travelers, business elites, and couples looking for a refined retreat in Vancouver’s bustling downtown core.

Rooms & Suites: Classic Charm with Modern Detail

Each of the 156 rooms and suites reflects the hotel’s commitment to understated luxury, featuring rich walnut furnishings, velvet accents, and Carrera marble bathrooms.

Signature Perks:

  • Deluxe Rooms start at 475 sq ft with walk-in rain showers, deep soaking tubs, and custom millwork.

  • Suites offer expansive layouts, skyline views, separate living areas, and personal butler services for Signature Suites.

Top Pick: The Lord Stanley Suite, with 1,280 sq ft of living space, a private rooftop terrace, fireplace, and outdoor plunge pool, is one of Vancouver’s most prestigious accommodations.

Signature Features:

  • High-thread-count Italian linens

  • In-room Nespresso machine

  • Heated bathroom floors and mirror-integrated TVs

  • Luxurious attention to lighting and acoustics

Dining: Refined, Seasonal, and Quietly Iconic

➤ Hawksworth Restaurant – Vancouver’s Fine Dining Standard

Frequently listed among Canada’s best restaurants, Hawksworth delivers seasonally inspired Canadian cuisine with a global twist. It’s the hotel’s culinary crown jewel and a destination in itself.

  • Must-Try: West Coast halibut, Alberta beef tenderloin, and the signature tastingmenu

  • Ambience: RElegant, modern, and understated—ideal for both romantic dinners and business lunches

➤ Reflections Terrace – Lush Urban Rooftop Vibes

Open seasonally, this outdoor oasis on the 4th floor combines light bites, cocktails, and ambient lighting among vertical gardens and fire pits.

Room Service offers 24-hour luxury dining with elevated comfort food options and wine pairings.

Wellness: Boutique Calm with a Wellness Edge

Sense, A Rosewood Spa – Intimate and Purposeful

Located on the 4th floor, the spa emphasizes personalized wellness through bespoke massage treatments and facialsusing natural, Canadian-sourced ingredients.

  • Highlights include the Georgia Custom Massage and Rosewood Facial

  • Guests also enjoy a 52-foot indoor saltwater lap pool, hot tub, and 24-hour fitness studio with Peloton bikes

TurboRoo Tip: Book the Vancouver Signature Ritual, which blends aromatherapy, hydrotherapy, and massage in a 90-minute experience..

Service & Experience: Discretion and Excellence

Expect white-glove, intuitive service across every touchpoint. From bellhop to butler, every staff member operates with quiet professionalism, deeply rooted in Rosewood’s "A Sense of Place" philosophy.

  • Concierge-led city experiences are available (private gallery tours, tailored dining routes)

  • Turndown service includes bottled water, mood lighting, and chocolate truffles

  • Chauffeur-driven Bentley available for local transport upon request

Location: Cultural Core of Downtown Vancouver

Rosewood Hotel Georgia sits on Georgia and Howe, just steps fro

  • Vancouver Art Gallery

  • Robson Street shopping

  • Pacific Centre Mall

  • Canada Place and Convention Centre

  • A short drive or walk to Stanley Park

It’s an unbeatable base for art lovers, luxury shoppers, and urban explorers alike.

What Makes It Exceptional

  • Flawless restoration of a 1920s landmark

  • Boutique luxury with a heritage soul

  • Michelin-standard dining at Hawksworth

  • Impeccably discreet service

  • Private rooftop plunge pools in select suites

  • Cultural proximity with total serenity inside

Final Verdict

Rosewood Hotel Georgia is where history meets contemporary class in Vancouver’s cultural heart. It offers boutique-style intimacy wrapped in five-star excellence, ideal for guests who value refinement, privacy, and subtle splendor.

  • Ideal For: Design aficionados, heritage enthusiasts, romantic couples, discreet VIPs

    Not For: Travelers looking for high-energy nightlife, large resort amenities, or edgy modernism
