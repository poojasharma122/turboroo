Four Seasons Hotel Montreal

A Flagship of Fashion, Fine Dining, and Five-Star Seduction

Hotel Snapshot

Four Seasons Hotel Montreal is a modern luxury oasis in the heart of the city’s chic Golden Square Mile. A perfect fusion of high fashion, sensual design, and cultural sophistication, this five-star flagship opened in 2019 and quickly positioned itself as one of Canada’s most visually striking and service-forward urban retreats.

Whether you're attending Montreal Fashion Week, closing a high-stakes deal, or seeking a romantic escape, this hotel delivers curated elegance in every corner— elevated by the brand’s hallmark of flawless service.

Rooms & Suites: Sleek, Romantic, and High-Tech

The hotel features 169 rooms and suites, all designed with a minimal yet luxurious aesthetic: soft blush tones, matte black fixtures, custom lighting, and velvet textures. Floor-to-ceiling windows reveal sweeping city or mountain views.

Signature Perks:

  • Deluxe Rooms: Bright and open with spa-style bathrooms, integrated mirror TVs, and marble vanities.

  • Suites: Include spacious living areas, walk-in closets, and custom minibars curated with local delicacies.

Top Pick: The Presidential Suite—an ultra-modern apartment with two terraces, a dining room, and expansive skyline views—is ideal for private events or luxury stays with impact.

In-Room Highlights:

  • Voice-controlled lighting and climate systems

  • Deep soaking tubs with city-view options

  • Custom Four Seasons bedding for cloud-like sleep

  • Oversized workstations and touchscreen concierge tablets

Dining: Haute Cuisine, Curated Cocktails, and Culinary Drama

➤ Marcus Restaurant + Lounge – Flagship Dining by Chef Marcus Samuelsson

Marcus is more than a hotel restaurant—it’s a standalone dining destination. With its art deco-meets-industrial-chic design and buzzing energy, it’s equally appealing for power lunches and candlelit dinners.

  • Must-Try: Montreal-style salmon crudo, crispy duck confit, and the signature Marcus cocktail

  • Terrace: Seasonal rooftop dining with views over downtown Montreal

➤ Marcus Bar – Crafted, Stylish, and Social

A glamorous bar scene popular with locals and hotel guests, known for its DJ sets, mixology-forward cocktails, and dramatic marble bar.

Room Service is also elevated, with 24-hour fine dining, including late-night charcuterie boards and champagne pairings.

➤ Rundle Bar – Social Centerpiece with Craft Cocktails

Stunning mountain views, a dramatic spiral staircase, and nightly music make this bar the heart of the hotel's après-ski scene.

Other Notables:

  • Grapes Wine Bar for charcuterie and private tastings

  • Stanley’s Smokehouse (seasonal) on the golf course with panoramic vistas

  • Afternoon Tea in the Castle Lounge—an elegant nod to the hotel’s British roots

Spa & Wellness: World-Class Indulgence with Alpine Energy

Willow Stream Spa – Mountain Therapy at Its Finest

Regularly named one of Canada’s top resort spas, Willow Stream features 27,000 sq ft of tranquil pools, steam rooms, and treatment sanctuaries.

  • Signature Treatments: Alpine Aromatherapy Massage, Rocky Mountain Mud Wrap, and mineral soaks

  • Facilities:

    • Outdoor heated pool and indoor lap pool

    • Eucalyptus inhalation room

    • Private lounges and spa terrace with stunning views

Fitness facilities and yoga classes round out the wellness experience.

Service: Attentive and Experienced

Despite its size, Fairmont Banff Springs delivers personalized and polished service, especially in the Gold Floors and Signature Suites. Concierge staff are knowledgeable about both the resort and national park excursions.

  • Valet parking and shuttle service within Banff

  • In-room dining available 24/7

  • Fairmont Gold Service includes private check-in, lounge access, and daily canapés

TurboRoo Tip: Upgrade to the Fairmont Gold Experience for a quieter, more curated stay with added exclusivity.

Location: Nature’s Front Row Seat

Located just above the town of Banff, the hotel is perfectly situated for:

  • Hiking, biking, skiing, and wildlife tours

  • Banff Gondola and Hot Springs (minutes away)

  • Day trips to Lake Louise, Moraine Lake, and Icefields Parkway

Panoramic views in every direction, paired with easy access to town amenities, make this one of the best-located resorts in North America.

What Sets It Apart

  • Legendary castle architecture surrounded by wilderness

  • A full-scale resort experience with golf, spa, adventure, and cuisine

  • Over a century of luxury heritage

  • Unmatched mountain views and wildlife sightings

  • Popular year-round: ski season, summer hikes, and autumn retreats

Final Verdict

Fairmont Banff Springs is not just a luxury stay—it’s a Canadian rite of passage. Equal parts majestic and approachable, it offers a unique blend of heritage, grandeur, and alpine serenity. Guests leave not only refreshed—but inspired.

  • Ideal For: Couples, multigenerational families, outdoor enthusiasts, luxury history lovers

    Not For: Minimalist travelers or those seeking urban boutique experiences
